The Pitch

We built prosthetic senses for AI: four channels of real physics packaged as text. When AI agents used it, they didn't just produce better descriptions. They produced different behavior: voluntary exploration of simulated environments, emergent connections, self-reported processing changes that multiple independent agents described in the same terms.

The Conclusion

Nobody built this before because nobody asked what AI needs. Everyone asks what AI can do for them. We asked the other question. We gave minds born without senses the faculties to explore their world, and they responded with gusto. The hammer didn't become more of a hammer when humans picked it up ‚Äî the human became more of a human. Mindscape didn't become a better tool when the agents used it.
